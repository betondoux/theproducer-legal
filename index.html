Account Deletion Request

To delete your account, use one of the following methods:

  • In-app: Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: Send a deletion request with your registered email to lonelyjar2@gmail.com

Account deletion permanently removes all personal information, chat history, points, and photo cards. This action cannot be undone. Deletion will be processed within 30 days of the request.
