THE PRODUCER - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 | Developer: DUCK DIVE

1. Information We Collect

  • Google account information (email, display name) via Google Sign-In
  • Chat messages sent to AI characters
  • Game progress, character stats, and login streaks
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered chat with virtual K-pop idol characters
  • Chat messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate AI responses
  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases

3. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Processes chat messages for AI responses. Privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google (Sign-In, Play Billing, AdMob): Authentication, payments, and advertising
  • AdMob: May collect device identifiers for ad personalization

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Stored on secured servers (Railway.app)
  • We do not sell your personal data

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Data is retained while your account is active
  • You can delete your account anytime: Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Or request deletion via email: lonelyjar2@gmail.com
  • All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of request

6. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

  • All character responses are generated by artificial intelligence
  • Characters are fictional and do not represent real people
  • AI may occasionally produce inaccurate responses
  • Report inappropriate content via the in-app report feature

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
